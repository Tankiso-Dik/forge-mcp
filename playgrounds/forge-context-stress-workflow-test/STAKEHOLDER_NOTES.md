# Stakeholder Notes

These notes are intentionally messy and partly conflicting.

## Product Lead

- Keep release 1 tightly scoped.
- Local-first is a real differentiator.
- Weekly recovery flow should be the center of the experience.
- Avoid a feature matrix that turns the product into a project-management clone.

## Design Lead

- The UI should feel lightweight and confidence-building.
- Prefer one strong weekly view plus one detail view.
- Avoid too many system-generated statuses.
- Users should be able to mark uncertainty or “not sure yet.”

## Growth Advisor

- Consider email reminders because they improve retention.
- Consider account sync so users can access data across devices.
- Consider shareable weekly summaries for accountability.

## Engineering Lead

- Avoid external dependencies in the first release if possible.
- Keep operational surface area small.
- If sync exists, it should not compromise the local-first story.

## Operations

- Do not create a support burden around fragile cross-device sync in the first release.
- Recovery after partial data corruption matters more than fancy reminders.
